EAV

Energy Authentication and Verification

Calibrus offers a new and innovative service to the competitive energy industry called Energy Authentication and Verification (EAV). A substantial enhancement to traditional Third Party Verification, new client onboarding and ongoing customer retention services, EAV is designed to:


  • Decrease fraud and slamming, especially in residential door to door sales
  • Deliver high quality authentication and third party verification services designed to maximize sales results while maintaining the utmost process integrity
  • Provide credit check/authorization process
  • Enhance client onboarding processes by providing client survey and welcome programs
  • Interact with new systems and devices such as tablet based applications as well as legacy environments to maximize value and minimize expenses
  • Maintain overall business integrity
  • Minimize revenue loss
  • Stay ahead of the compliance curve
  • Increase customer retention rates
  • Ensure high Net Promoter Scores
