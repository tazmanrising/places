Authentication

Authentication – a key component of EAV - utilizes a comprehensive data authentication process to identify potential fraud activity by effectively scoring critical customer data elements against multiple data sources. It searches for and verifies critical information to monitor potential slamming and fraud items such as google/internet/throw away telephone numbers, global positioning service (GPS)/location related items, name/address/telephone/birthday/SSN validation and much more.


EAV authentication helps prevent:


  • Lost Customers
  • Lost Revenues
  • Lost Trust
  • Bad Press
  • Regulatory Fines
  • High Legal Costs
  • High Operational Costs
